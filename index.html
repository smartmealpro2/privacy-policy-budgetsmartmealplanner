2.3 Health and Fitness Data (Google Fit / Apple HealthKit Integration)

To provide personalized wellness tracking, Budget Smart Meal Planner enables users to connect their accounts with Google Fit (Android) or Apple HealthKit (iOS).

  • What we collect: With your explicit permission, we request read access exclusively to your daily Step Count data. We do not request, access, or track your GPS location, heart rate, or sleep data.
  • How we use it: We use your daily step count purely to provide our "Step Tracker & Fitness" app feature. Based on the steps retrieved, our app calculations locally estimate your daily distance walked and active calories burned. We compare these burned calories against your personalized meal plans to help you determine if you are maintaining a caloric deficit or surplus in accordance with your weight goals (e.g., Lose Weight or Gain Weight).
  • Protection and Sharing: Your health and step data is retrieved securely and is never sold, licensed, or shared with third-party advertisers or external brokers. You may revoke the app's access to this data at any time via your device's native health settings. Our use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
